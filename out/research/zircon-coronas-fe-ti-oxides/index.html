ZIRCON CORONAS AROUND FE-TI OXIDES

Temporal Constraints from Zircon Corona Textures

Research Overview

Zircon coronas around Fe-Ti oxide minerals provide unique geochronological constraints on metamorphic and igneous processes. Our research develops new approaches to date these complex textural relationships.

Geochronological Methods

We use high-resolution ion microprobe techniques to date zircon coronas, providing precise temporal constraints on the formation and evolution of Fe-Ti oxide-bearing assemblages.

Textural Analysis

Detailed petrographic and electron microscopy studies reveal the complex relationships between zircon coronas and their host Fe-Ti oxides, informing our geochronological interpretations.
