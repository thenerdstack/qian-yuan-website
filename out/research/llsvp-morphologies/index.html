DIFFERENT MORPHOLOGIES OF LLSVPS

Structural Variations in Large Low Shear Velocity Provinces

Research Overview

LLSVPs exhibit distinct morphological characteristics that provide insights into their origin and evolution. Our research focuses on understanding the structural diversity of these deep mantle features.

Morphological Analysis

We analyze seismic tomography data to characterize the three-dimensional structure and morphology of LLSVPs, identifying key differences between the African and Pacific provinces.
